HOW MOBILE DETAILING WORKS

Simple, fast, and entirely on your schedule — we make it effortless to keep your car looking its best.

Step 1: Book Online

Scheduling is incredibly easy. You can schedule your car detailing online at any time. All pricing is available online and at booking — no guessing, no back-and-forth.

Step 2: We Come to You

No need to drive anywhere — we show up at your home, office, or marina and take care of everything right where your vehicle is parked.

Step 3: Pay Online

Our techs text when they're on the way. We send an invoice when your detail is finished — pay easily online when your service is complete.

Meet the Owner

Nathan & Koa

Hey, I'm Nathan — founder of Koa Pro Detail and a lifelong car enthusiast based in Charleston, SC. I started this business because I believed there was a better way to detail cars: no drop-offs, no waiting rooms, just pro-level results brought directly to your driveway.

Koa is my Husky mix and my co-pilot on every job. She's the heart behind the name — and a reminder that this business is built on genuine care, not just a transaction.

Every vehicle I touch gets treated like it's my own. Whether it's a daily driver or a show car, I bring the same attention to detail, premium products, and passion for perfection to every single appointment.
