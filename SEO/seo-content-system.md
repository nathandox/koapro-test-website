## 1. Research Summary

### Competitive Landscape

The Charleston mobile detailing market is fragmented. Most competitors rely on Google Business Profiles and word of mouth — very few publish consistent blog content. This is the primary opportunity.

**Weak spots identified across local competitors:**
- Little to no long-form educational content
- No location-specific landing pages or blog posts targeting suburbs (Daniel Island, Kiawah, Summerville)
- No content targeting marine/boat detailing, which is a real Lowcountry niche with almost zero local coverage
- No comparison content (mobile vs. shop, ceramic vs. wax)
- No content addressing Charleston-specific vehicle threats: salt air, UV intensity, humidity

**Koa Pro Detail's advantages to lean into:**
- 110+ Google reviews — a trust signal competitors can't match quickly
- Mobile-only model — a real differentiator worth explaining repeatedly
- Marine detailing — almost no one covers this in content
- Multi-suburb coverage — enables a full cluster of location-specific posts
- Luxury vehicle positioning (Tesla, Porsche, Audi in photo assets)
