Frequently Asked Questions

An interior or exterior detail typically takes 1.5–3 hours depending on vehicle size and condition. A full detail runs 2.5–4 hours. We'll give you an accurate time estimate when you book.

No. Most customers meet us at the start, hand over the keys, and go about their day. We text when we're on the way and send an invoice when we're done. You can pay online — no need to be present the whole time.

An interior detail focuses entirely on the inside of your vehicle — vacuuming, wipe-downs, leather conditioning, and window cleaning. An exterior detail covers the paint, wheels, trim, and glass on the outside, including a clay bar treatment and ceramic sealant. A full detail combines both for a complete transformation.

In the Lowcountry, we recommend a full detail every 3–6 months due to salt air, UV exposure, and humidity. If you drive daily or have pets and kids, an interior detail every 2–3 months keeps your cabin in great shape.

We accept cash, card, and Zelle — whatever is most convenient for you. We send an invoice when your detail is complete and you can pay online.
